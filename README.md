# Dirt Inc.

A browser recreation of **Dirt Inc.** by Digital Panda Studios, the pixel-art idle
digging game for Android and iOS (2014-2019, now delisted).

**Play it:** open `index.html` in any browser. No build step, no dependencies -
the whole game is a single self-contained HTML file.

## How to play

- Tap the screen to dig. It takes 3 taps to break a block at first.
- Special blocks are worth more dirt: Coal 2x, Copper 4x, Silver 6x, Gold 11x, Diamond 100x.
- Gnome blocks spawn a gnome worker who delivers the value of 5 random blocks.
- Crates drop items: Tools, Helmets, Gloves, Boots and Jackets in six rarities
  (white, green, blue, purple, yellow, red). Red items form the "of Digging" and
  "of Production" sets with powerful 5/5 bonuses.

## Systems

- **Upgrades** (right tab): Tap, Digger, Refinery, Foreman, Gnomes, the Generator
  with its boost tiers, the Energy Tap, and the five repeatable percentage upgrades.
- **Quarry**: six buildable mines (Dirt, Coal, Copper, Silver, Gold, Diamond) that
  generate dirt on timers.
- **Research**: eight one-time upgrades.
- **Energy events**: Tap Lord, Hyper Quarry, Laser Drill, Power Gnome.
- **Contracts** (left tab): three rotating goals with crate rewards and re-rolls.
- **Achievements**: seven categories, six tiers each.
- **Prestige**: full reset in exchange for permanent bonus base dirt.
- Day/night cycle, offline earnings, and world progression from Topsoil to the
  Dirt Dimension.

Progress saves automatically to your browser's local storage.

## Notes

All art and code in this repository are original, drawn and written from scratch
to recreate the look and mechanics of the original game. Dirt Inc. is a game by
Digital Panda Studios; this is a fan recreation, not an official release.
